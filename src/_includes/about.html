About

"America is Going Just Great" is a website that curates a timeline of events highlighting America's descent into a fascist hellscape. The site aims to inform and engage readers by providing a chronological account of actions and policies that reflect this decline. Through the checklist at the top, you can filter by interest (if you only want to see the good news, filter out everything but 'Resist'). Please see the note on "Fascism" and "Project 2025" on the Resources page. Red icons indicate troubling or frightening events while green icons depict acts of resistence. (For those who are red/green color blind, the green icons have a slight drop shadow so you can tell the difference.)

Since these articles are compiled by one person, it's proven impossible to keep up with the barrage of daily articles during this terrifying time. This list has become unmanagably long, often resulting in hours every day. As of 18 April 2025, the focus will be on only the most egregious actions and acts of resistence. Other than a few exceptions, only one source is listed, although there are likely many others. Please feel free to suggest articles as well as report any mistakes directly to Christine. You can also create an issue or PR on the GitHub Repo.
